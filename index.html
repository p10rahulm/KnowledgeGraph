Graphing the knowledge graph: distance between concepts

The three concept words chosen here are: a) Hubble Deep Field b) House of Lords and c) Ichthyosaur

Hover over a vertex to see the page name. Note 3 distinct regions in the graph

Here we have taken the wikipedia links data.

The steps in constructing the graph are as follows:

  • We choose one word to start from the word space
  • We choose all the words that link from the wikipedia page of this word (back into wikipedia). Let us call this the concept space.
  • We choose the subset of these links that link back into our concept space. This is reinforcement that the word belongs to the concept space.
  • We repeat for any number of words we like.
  • Note that the hubs linking between regions may act as bridges
